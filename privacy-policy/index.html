Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Archivas (we, our, or us) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and related services at archivas.ai.

We designed Archivas to operate transparently, with minimal data collection and no invasive tracking. Please read this policy carefully to understand what data we collect, how its used, and what rights you have.

  1. Information We Collect
    We do not collect any personal data, cookies, or analytics information by default.

You may voluntarily provide data if you:
Contact us via email or community channels (e.g., Discord, Telegram)
Interact with third-party links (e.g., GitHub repositories)

Any information you share is used solely to respond to your inquiries or improve the community experience.

  1. On-Chain Data
    When using any blockchain-related features, the only data recorded is your public wallet address and on-chain interactions. This information is stored on the blockchain itself, which is public and not controlled by Archivas.

We do not have access to your private keys or wallet credentials.

  1. Third-Party Links
    Archivas may link to third-party platforms such as:
    GitHub (for open-source code).
    Discord or Telegram (for community discussions).
    Block explorers or analytics dashboards.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their respective policies before interacting with them.

  1. Data Retention
    We only keep information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was provided for, typically until your inquiry or issue is resolved.

  2. Security
    We apply reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect any voluntarily submitted data. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

  3. Your Rights
    If youve shared information with us (for example, through our support email), you may request deletion at any time by contacting [email protected].

  4. Updates
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
